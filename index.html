The full picture

Two careers.
One story.

Most people know me as one thing. At EY, I lead complex technology transformations — AI-driven platforms, cloud modernization, developer experience — for organizations that need to move faster without breaking what already works.

But I also founded Motherhood by Design, a coaching initiative that helps high-achieving mothers return to work without losing themselves in the process. The two are more connected than they look.

The skills that make me effective at EY — systems thinking, sustainable performance, redesigning what isn't working — are exactly the skills I bring to the mothers I work with. The difference is the scale of the problem.

This site is where both live. Because separating them was never the answer.
