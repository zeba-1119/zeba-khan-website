About

The full story.

I grew up believing you had to choose. Career or family. Ambition or peace. The version of success the world hands you, or the one you actually want.

I chose not to choose.

That decision — made not once but daily, over 19 years — is what this page is about.

The career

19 years. One relentless forward motion.

I started in tech as a performance test lead, then moved into cloud engineering at Capital One, then into consulting at EY — where I've spent the last 8 years leading some of the most complex technology transformations in the industry.

AI-driven platforms. Cloud modernization. Developer experience. FinOps. I've been a Governing Board Member of the FinOps Foundation. I hold an MIT Sloan AI certification and a Harvard graduate certificate in software engineering. In 2025, I was named a Leader of Impact by Women Impact Tech.

I love this work. I love the complexity of it, the scale of it, the fact that what I help build actually changes how organizations operate. I'm not leaving it behind. I'm building alongside it.

The businesses

Building things from the ground up.

In 2025 I founded Motherhood by Design — a coaching initiative for high-achieving mothers navigating the return to work. Not the logistics. The whole thing: identity, confidence, body, relationships, ambition, and what success looks like now.

I also built a second business from the ground up — because I believe in building enterprises that generate income and serve communities, and because I wanted to practice what I coach. Building something while holding a senior role and raising a daughter is exactly the kind of challenge I help other women navigate.

Building is not a side hustle for me. It is part of what I am.

The life

Designed, not defaulted into.

I have been to 30+ countries. I have a daughter who told me she wants to be an astronaut first, then a mama. I have a husband who stepped in fully so I could speak on stage about the mothers who feel they have to hide what they are. I meditate, inconsistently. I eat well, most of the time. I am building the financial freedom to control my time — not someday, but now, deliberately.

Travel is not my reward for working hard. It is how I think. It is where my best ideas come from. It is non-negotiable.

Why this site

The whole story is the credential.

For a long time, I kept these worlds separate. The tech leader at EY. The founder. The mother. The traveler. As if combining them would make me less credible in each.

The opposite is true.

A woman who has navigated a 19-year career at the highest level, built businesses on the side, raised a daughter with intention, and kept choosing joy — that is the person you want in your corner.

That's who I am. This site is where I say it out loud.
