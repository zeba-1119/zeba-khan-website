About

The full story.

19 years in tech. A senior role at EY. A business built from the ground up. A daughter who wants to be an astronaut first. 55+ countries. One deliberate life.

The career

19 years. One relentless forward motion.

From performance test lead to cloud engineering at Capital One to 8 years at EY leading complex technology transformations — AI platforms, cloud modernisation, developer experience, FinOps. Governing Board Member of the FinOps Foundation. MIT Sloan AI and Harvard Software Engineering certifications. Named a Leader of Impact by Women Impact Tech in 2025.

Speaking has been part of that work too — at GitHub Universe, Women Impact Tech, and EY panels on elevating women in technology and redefining what modern leadership requires.

The businesses

Building things from the ground up.

Motherhood by Design was founded in 2025 — a coaching initiative for high-achieving mothers navigating the return to work. Not the logistics. The whole picture: identity, confidence, body, relationships, and what success actually looks like now.

Two businesses built alongside a senior role and raising a daughter — because building enterprises that generate income and serve communities is something worth practising, not just preaching.

The life

Designed, not defaulted into.

55+ countries. A daughter who wants to be an astronaut first. A partnership built on full presence. Financial freedom as a present-tense goal, not a future one. Travel not as reward but as oxygen — where the best thinking happens.

Why this site

The whole story is the credential.

For a long time these worlds were kept separate — as if combining them would make each less credible. The opposite is true. A 19-year career at the highest level, businesses built alongside it, a daughter raised with intention, joy kept deliberately — that is the person you want in your corner.

This site is where it's all said out loud.
