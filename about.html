About

The full story.

The early message was clear: choose. Career or family. Ambition or peace. The version of success the world hands you, or the one you actually want.

Choosing not to choose was the answer.

That decision — made not once but daily, over 19 years — is what this page is about.

The career

19 years. One relentless forward motion.

The career started as a performance test lead, moved into cloud engineering at Capital One, then into consulting at EY — where the last 8 years have been spent leading some of the most complex technology transformations in the industry.

AI-driven platforms. Cloud modernization. Developer experience. FinOps. Governing Board Member of the FinOps Foundation. MIT Sloan AI certification and a Harvard graduate certificate in software engineering. Named a Leader of Impact by Women Impact Tech in 2025.

Speaking has been part of that work too — at GitHub Universe, Women Impact Tech, and EY panels focused on elevating women in technology and accelerating the conversation about what modern leadership actually requires.

This work demands everything — the complexity, the scale, the fact that what gets built actually changes how organizations operate. Not leaving it behind. Building alongside it.

The businesses

Building things from the ground up.

Motherhood by Design was founded in 2025 — a coaching initiative for high-achieving mothers navigating the return to work. Not the logistics. The whole thing: identity, confidence, body, relationships, ambition, and what success looks like now.

A second business was built from the ground up alongside it — because building enterprises that generate income and serve communities is something worth practicing, not just preaching. Building something while holding a senior role and raising a daughter is exactly the kind of challenge this work helps other women navigate.

Building is not a side hustle. It is part of the work.

The life

Designed, not defaulted into.

55+ countries. A daughter who said she wants to be an astronaut first, then a mama. A husband who stepped in fully so there was room to speak on stage about the mothers who feel they have to hide what they are. Meditating, inconsistently. Eating well, most of the time. Building the financial freedom to control time — not someday, but now, deliberately.

Travel is not a reward for working hard. It's how thinking happens. Where the best ideas come from. Non-negotiable.

Why this site

The whole story is the credential.

For a long time, these worlds were kept separate. The tech leader at EY. The founder. The mother. The traveler. As if combining them would make each less credible.

The opposite is true.

A woman who has navigated a 19-year career at the highest level, built businesses alongside a senior role, raised a daughter with intention, and kept choosing joy — that is the person you want in your corner.

That's the whole story. This site is where it's said out loud.
